First Come First Serve (FCFS)

First Come First Serve (FCFS) is the simplest CPU scheduling algorithm that schedules processes according to their arrival time.

Key Characteristics:

  • Non-preemptive algorithm
  • Processes are executed in order of arrival
  • Simple to implement but may result in long waiting times
  • Convoy effect may occur (short process behind long process)
